About US

An Institute for Placement Preparation

At AARAMBHYA, we empower engineering students to foster their technical & soft skills and provide them the required assistance to make them Industry ready. By giving them especially curated programs and leveraging industry expert's exposure, we help them grow in their knowledge, confidence, and help them consumed in the IT industry.

We are committed to bridge the gap between young mind of the state & the world outside their campus to be able to pursue their dreams.
AARAMBHYA has taken the initiative to start a specialized training program on Campus Placement preparation & various demanding courses in IT industry.
